🎅 Secret Santa Guessing Game

Race to discover who your Secret Santa is!

Host a Game Join a Game

How to Play:

  1. Host creates a game and shares the room code
  2. Players join and enter who they're gifting to
  3. Once everyone joins, the host starts the game
  4. Race to guess who YOUR Secret Santa is!
  5. Fewest guesses wins! 🏆
